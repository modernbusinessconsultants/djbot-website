About DJ BOT

I'm Bryce LeDuc, founder of DJ BOT, right here in Beaumont, TX. I started DJ'ing in 2021, driven by a deep passion for music and a desire to make every event unforgettable. What began as a love for mixing beats and reading a crowd has grown into Southeast Texas's go-to DJ service for school dances, private parties, corporate gatherings, weddings, and everything in between.

I believe every event deserves a soundtrack that fits like a glove. Whether you are walking down the aisle, celebrating a milestone birthday, or hosting a company gala, I bring professional-grade sound equipment, dynamic lighting, and a versatile music library spanning every genre and era. From country to hip-hop, from classic rock to today's biggest hits, I curate the perfect mix for your crowd.

The "BOT" in DJ BOT is more than a name, it is a vibe. Think cutting-edge technology meets human creativity. My robotic mascot represents the precision and reliability I bring to every gig, paired with the energy and adaptability that only a live DJ can deliver. I show up on time, set up seamlessly, and keep the dance floor packed from the first song to the last.

Based in Beaumont and proudly serving the Golden Triangle, greater Houston, Lake Charles, and beyond, DJ BOT is ready to bring the beat to your next event. Let me handle the music so you can focus on making memories.
